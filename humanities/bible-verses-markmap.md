## Secular power

- Render to Caesar
  - Matthew 22
  - Context
    - Pharisees testing Jesus
    - "Why do you put me to the test, you hypocrites? Show me the coin for the tax."
    - They brought him a denarius
    - "Whose likeness and inscription is this?"
    - "Caesar's," they said
  - Teaching
    - "Therefore render to Caesar the things that are Caesar's, and to God the things that are God's."
  - Separation of
    - Secular authority
    - Religious duty

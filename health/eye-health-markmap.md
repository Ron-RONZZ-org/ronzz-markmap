# Eye Health

## Screen

- Computer Vision Syndrome (CVS)
  - [WebMD article](https://www.webmd.com/eye-health/computer-vision-syndrome)
- Myopia, presbyopia
  - [UNSW research](https://www.unsw.edu.au/newsroom/news/2024/01/is-too-much-screen-time-bad-for-our-eyes-)

## Take action

- 20-20-20 rule
  - Every 20 minutes
  - Look at least 20 meters away
  - For 20 seconds
- More outdoor activities
- Eye examination every two years

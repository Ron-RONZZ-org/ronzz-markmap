# Computer Vision Syndrome

## Overview

- CVS
  - Eye strain from computer use
  - Not one specific problem
  - Range of symptoms
- Prevalence
  - 50-90% of computer users
  - Working adults
  - Children using tablets/computers

## How computers affect vision

- Repetitive eye motion
  - Similar to carpal tunnel
  - Eyes follow same path repeatedly
  - Focus and refocus constantly
- Screen challenges
  - Contrast
  - Flicker
  - Glare
  - Moving images
- Reduced blinking
  - Eyes dry out
  - Vision blurs periodically
  - Halved blink rate

## Risk factors

- Existing eye trouble
- No glasses when needed
- Wrong prescription for computer use
- Age-related changes
  - Age 40+: Presbyopia
  - Lens flexibility decreases
  - Focus ability diminishes

## Symptoms

- No long-term damage proven
- Regular use causes
  - Blurred vision
  - Double vision
  - Dry, red eyes
  - Eye irritation
  - Headaches
  - Neck or back pain
- Work performance impact

## Treatment

### Workspace adjustments

- Cut glare
  - Adjust lighting
  - Close window shades
  - Dimmer switch for fixtures
  - Desk lamp with moveable shade
  - Glare filter for monitor
- Rearrange desk
  - Monitor position
    - Slightly below eye level
    - 20-28 inches from face
  - Stand for printed materials
  - Avoid neck strain
- Give eyes a break
  - 20-20-20 rule
    - Every 20 minutes
    - Look 20 feet away
    - For 20 seconds
  - Blink often
  - Artificial tears if dry
- Tweak settings
  - Brightness
  - Contrast
  - Font size
  - Adjust to comfort

### Medical care

- Regular eye exams
- Update prescriptions
- Inform doctor of problems
- Glasses options
  - Regular glasses
  - Computer-specific glasses
  - Single or bifocal lens
  - Tinted lens material
    - Boost contrast
    - Filter glare

### Children

- Regular eye checkups
- Proper computer setup
  - Right height
  - Good lighting
- Frequent breaks
- Limit screen time
  - WHO guidelines
    - < 2 hours/day (school-age)
    - No screen time (under 2)

## Prevention

- Limit screen time
- Follow ergonomic guidelines
- Desktop screen
  - Appropriate distance
  - Proper height
- Adequate natural lighting
- Regular breaks
- 20-20-20 rule
